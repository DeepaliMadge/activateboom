Unlocked Devices

What is unlocked devices?
An unlocked cell phone or tablet is not tied to any specific wireless carrier. This means you can use it with any wireless provider, as long as they have a similar network. (Purchase of a service plan and a SIM card required). Most cell phones and tablets that use SIM cards are sold locked, which means they are programmed to only work with the original carrier. To get a device unlocked, contact the original wireless carrier. They will usually unlock it for you as long as you meet the requirements.
How do I know if my phone is unlocked?
To test your phone, try borrowing a SIM card from a friend who's on a different wireless carrier and insert it into your phone. If you are able to make and receive calls, then your phone is probably unlocked.* *boom!
